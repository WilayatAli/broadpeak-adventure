OverView

Explore the captivating Hunza Valley in Pakistan, beginning in Islamabad and its iconic landmarks. Drive along the Karakoram Highway, stopping to admire Shatial Petroglyphs. In the heart of Hunza Valley, visit Baltit Fort, Altit Fort, and the serene Shishkin Garden. Explore local handicrafts at Karimabad Bazaar. Discover the striking Attabad Lake, and the picturesque Hussaini Suspension Bridge, and make an exhilarating day trip to Khunjerab Pass. Depending on the time, visit Naltar Valley before concluding your journey in Islamabad.

Itinerary

Day - 1: Arrival in Islamabad

Your enchanting 7-day Hunza tour begins in Islamabad, Pakistan's capital. Check into your accommodation and unwind after your journey.
Time permitting, explore Islamabad's landmarks like Faisal Mosque, Daman-e-Koh, and Lok Virsa Museum.

Day - 2: Islamabad to Chilas

Set out early on a picturesque drive along the world-renowned Karakoram Highway to Chilas.
Marvel at the awe-inspiring Shatial Petroglyphs and the convergence of the Karakoram, Himalayas, and Hindu Kush mountain ranges.
Rest and rejuvenate in Chilas for the night.

Day - 3: Chilas to Hunza Valley

Continue your scenic journey along the Karakoram Highway, taking in the rugged beauty and iconic Nanga Parbat.
Arrive in the heart of Hunza Valley, Karimabad, and settle into your accommodation. Explore the historical gems of Baltit Fort and Baltit Village.

Day - 4: Exploring Karimabad

Immerse yourself in the charm of Hunza Valley.
Visit Altit Fort, stroll through Shishkin Garden, and shop for local crafts at Karimabad Bazaar.
As the day winds down, savor the awe-inspiring views of Rakaposhi and Ultar Sar mountains.

Day - 5: Attabad Lake and Gojal Valley

Venture to the mesmerizing Attabad Lake, known for its turquoise waters resulting from a 2010 landslide.
Enjoy a boat ride on the lake to absorb its unique beauty.
Explore the picturesque Hussaini Suspension Bridge in Gojal Valley.
Return to Karimabad for a well-deserved rest.

Day - 6: Khunjerab Pass (Pak-China Border)

Embark on an epic day trip to Khunjerab Pass, marking the Pakistan-China border. Traverse Khunjerab National Park, offering wildlife-spotting opportunities. Gaze in wonder at Khunjerab Top, the highest point on the Karakoram Highway. Return to Karimabad for an evening of relaxation.

Day: 7: Departure to Islamabad

Bid farewell to Karimabad and journey back to Chilas or Gilgit.

Conclude your unforgettable Hunza tour as you return to Islamabad, marking the end of this incredible journey.
