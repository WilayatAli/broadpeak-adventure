OverView

Explore the Rush Lake Trek, a perfect adventure for beginners in the stunning Karakoram Range. Rush Lake, one of the world's highest alpine lakes and Pakistan's highest, sits majestically at over 4,700 meters.

During the trek, soak in the breathtaking views of Nagar and Hunza Valley's towering peaks. As you hike towards Rush Lake, you'll cross glaciers, meander through high meadows, and pass terraced fields in Nagar Valley. It's a refreshing journey, immersing you in the beauty of nature's wilderness.

Highlights

  • Trek to Rush Lake, one of the world's highest lakes, near the majestic Rush Pari peak.

  • Enjoy panoramic views of Nagar Valley's iconic peaks, including Spantik and Phuparash.

  • Marvel at the surrounding beauty, with glimpses of Hopper and Miar Glaciers.

  • Witness breathtaking vistas of the Karakoram Mountains, potentially including K2 Summit on clear days.

Itinerary

Day - 1: Arrive in Islamabad, transfer to centrally located hotel.

Day - 2: Drive to Chilas, stop at Shatial rock carving, overnight stay.

Day - 3: Continue to Karimabad Hunza, visit mountain range junction, check-in.

Day - 4: Explore Baltit and Altit forts, trek along Barbar water channel.

Day - 5: Jeep journey to Hopper Valley, overnight in simple lodge.

Day - 6: Trek to Barpo Giram campsite, stunning views of peaks.

Day - 7: Cross Barpu Glacier to Brikchour campsite.

Day - 8: Steep climb to Rush Lake, camping at Chiden Haral.

Day - 9: Free morning, descent to Gutons, overnight stay.

Day - 10: Return to Hopper village, drive to Gulmit, visit Attabad Lake.

Day - 11: Visit suspension bridges, Passu white glacier viewpoint, village tour.

Day - 12: Full-day excursion to Khunjerab Pass, wildlife spotting.

Day - 13: Travel to Islamabad via scenic flight or Karakoram Highway, stay in Besham.

Day - 14: Visit Islamabad’s last minute attractions , farewell dinner (nighit in Islamabad)

Day - 15: Departure
