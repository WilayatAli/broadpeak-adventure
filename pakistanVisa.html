Pakistan Visa Information

Foreign nationals are required to obtain a visa for entry into Pakistan, along with a passport that must remain valid for at least six months beyond their intended stay in the country.

Pakistan has recently introduced an online visa system, providing convenience for travellers seeking tourist, student, family, pilgrimage, and various other visa types. If you’re planning a tourist visit to Pakistan, you have three convenient options to apply for a Pakistan tourist visa:

1- On Arrival Tourist Visa (e-Visa):

You can apply for an on-arrival visa (e-Visa) if your country is on the list of eligible countries for Pakistan e-Visas. For your convenience, we’ve provided a list of these countries below. Please ensure your country is on this list to apply for an on-arrival visa. We highly recommend planning your Pakistan visit and initiating the visa application process as early as possible. The latest recommended time for an on-arrival visa application is 48-72 hours before your planned travel to receive your Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA).

Documents Required:

Passport, Photograph, Invitation Letter (for individual tourists) or Invitation Letter from a Tour Operator (for group tourists).

Validity and Duration:

The Tourist Visa on Arrival is typically valid for 30 days. Additionally, nationals from Malaysia, Turkey, and the UAE may be granted a 30-day, multiple-entry visa upon arrival.

Processing Time:

48-72 hours during working hours.

How to Apply for Pakistan On-Arrival Visa:

  1. Preliminary Assessment:

    Choose the appropriate visa type and understand the eligibility and document requirements.

  2. Preparation:

    Ensure you have a complete set of supporting documents, the required photograph, and a valid credit or debit card for online payment.

  3. Online Application:

    Follow these steps when you’re ready to apply:

    • Click “Apply Now” to access the Pakistan Online Visa System.
    • Create a new account.
    • Log in to the Pakistan Online Visa System.
    • Complete your application.
    • Pay the application fee using a credit or debit card.
    • Submit your application.

Please note that a detailed step-by-step guide is available here for assistance in using the official Pakistan Online Visa System.

PAKISTAN ONLINE TOURIST VISA

You have the option to apply for an online tourist visa for Pakistan if you hold nationality from one of the following countries. Please review the list below to confirm your eligibility.

Documents Required:

  • Valid Passport
  • Passport Photograph
  • Invitation Letter from a sponsor or hotel booking details for individual tourists

Validity and Duration:

  • Single Entry for short durations less than 3 months (for the first entry)
  • Duration can extend up to and beyond 6 months (with approval)
  • Citizens of these countries may be eligible for a Double Entry Tourist Visa.
  • Tourist Visas can be extended for up to 6 months or more, subject to approval.
  • US nationals can avail a Tourist 5-year multiple-entry visa with a maximum 3-month stay.

Processing Time:

  • Entry (Up to 3 Months): 7-10 working days
  • Extension (Up to 6 Months): 7-10 working days
  • Extension (Greater than 6 Months): 4 weeks (working days)
  • Tourist Visas can be extended for up to 6 months or more, subject to approval.
  • US nationals can avail a Tourist 5-year multiple-entry visa with a maximum 3-month stay.

How to Apply for Pakistan Visa Online:

1.Preliminary Assessment:
  • Choose the appropriate “Pakistan Visa” type and understand the eligibility and document requirements before you initiate your application.
    • 2.Preparation:
  • Ensure you’re applying for the correct visa type, as each category has specific requirements.
    • Have the following ready before you begin your visa application:
  • Complete set of supporting documents.
  • Passport photograph up to 350KB, as per the provided guidelines.
  • A valid credit card or debit card for online payment of your application fee.
    • 3.Online Application:
  • When you’re ready to apply, follow these steps:
  • Click on the “Apply Now” button to access the Pakistan OnlineVisa System.
  • Create a new account.
  • Log in to the Pakistan Online Visa System.
  • Complete your application.
  • Pay the application fee using a credit card or debit card.
  • Submit your application.
