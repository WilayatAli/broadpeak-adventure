OverView

Skardu, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Pakistan’s northern region, offers a unique blend of natural beauty and rich culture. A trip to Skardu is a must-do for those seeking an off-the-beaten-path adventure. Explore the ancient Skardu Fort, marvel at the crystal clear waters of Upper Kachura Lake, and discover the traditional way of life in the remote Shigar Valley. Take a jeep safari to the Deosai Plains, known for its diverse wildlife and picturesque landscape. Indulge in the delicious local cuisine and experience the warmth of the friendly locals. Skardu is the gateway to some of the world’s highest peaks, including K2 and Nanga Parbat, making it a perfect base for trekking and mountaineering. Plan your Skardu trip now and experience the beauty of Pakistan’s northern region like never before.

Itinerary

Day - 1: Arrival in Islamabad

Upon arriving at the Islamabad International airport, you will be met by the team of Broad Peak Adventures. They will assist you with your luggage and escort you to a hotel located in the heart of Islamabad.

Day - 2: Flight from Islamabad to Skardu and transfer to hotel

We will start the day early and take a flight that offers stunning views as we make our way to Skardu, the capital of Baltistan. Once we arrive, we will check in at the hotel and rest for a bit. After that, we will visit Kachura Lake, a picturesque location where you can take a boat ride. Afterwards, we will drive to Skardu town where you can take a leisurely stroll through the old bazaars. The evening will be spent overnight in Skardu.

Day - 3: Visit to Kharpocho Fort, and Sarfaranga Cold Desert

You will visit the Skardu Fort, which is an ancient fortress built in the 16th century, it offers a panoramic view of the Skardu valley. In the afternoon, you will drive to Sarfaranga Cold Desert and back to hotel in Skardu

Day - 4: Drive to Deosai Plains and overnight stay in a camp

You will drive to Deosai Plains, it is a high altitude plain known for its wildflowers during spring and snow leopards in winter.
You will spend the night in a camp in the middle of the Deosai Plains, where you can enjoy the starry night sky, and the peaceful silence of the high altitude.

Day - 5: Trek to Sheosar Lake and overnight stay in a camp

You will start the trek to Sheosar Lake, it is a beautiful lake surrounded by high peaks, it is a moderate to challenging trek, but the views are worth it. You will spend the night in a camp near the lake, where you can enjoy the beautiful scenery and the peaceful atmosphere.

Day - 6: Drive to Shigar and explore the surrounding

You will drive back to Shigar and visit the Shigar Fort, it's an ancient fortress built in the 17th century, it offers a great view of the Shigar valley.

In the evening, you will drive back to Skardu.

Day - 7: Drive to Khaplu and visit to Chaqchan Mosque and Yarmaq Tso Lake

You will drive to Khaplu, a small town known for its traditional architecture, you will visit the Chaqchan Mosque, an ancient mosque built in the 14th century.
You will also visit the Yarmaq Tso Lake, it is a beautiful lake surrounded by high peaks, you can take a boat ride or just relax.
Stay overnight in Khaplu Valley.

Day - 8 Drive to Hushe Valley from Khaplu

We will begin our day with breakfast and then set off towards Hushe Valley, one of the highest villages in the world, situated at an elevation of 3,050m. The valley is home to Masherbrum Mountain, which stands at 7,821m and is the 24th highest mountain in the world. We will spend the day hiking around Hushe and visiting the village, observing the culture and way of life. We will have lunch in Hushe and return to Khaplu in the evening. We will spend the night in Khalpu Valley.

Day - 9: Drive back to Skardu

Drive back to Skardu, transfer to hotel and rest. You can also spend some time shopping for souvenirs and local handicrafts.

Day - 10: Departure from Skardu
