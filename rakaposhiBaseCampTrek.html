OverView

Embark on an unforgettable journey to the base camp of Rakaposhi, one of the most beautiful mountains in Pakistan. The Rakaposhi base camp trek offers an opportunity to explore the stunning landscapes of the Karakoram Range while trekking through traditional villages and lush green valleys. The trek starts in the town of Minapin and takes you through the beautiful Rupal valley to the base camp of Rakaposhi. Along the way, you will have the opportunity to see glaciers, lakes and rivers, and enjoy the stunning views of Rakaposhi and other peaks of the Karakoram. The trek is led by experienced local guides and offers a unique and authentic experience of Pakistan’s natural beauty, culture and history. Book your Rakaposhi base camp trek now and discover the hidden gems of Pakistan’s Karakoram Range.

Itinerary

Day - 1: Arrival in Islamabad and transfer to hotel

Day - 2: Fly/Drive to Gilgit, the gateway to the Rakaposhi base camp trek

Day - 3: Drive to Minapin, the starting point of the trek

Day - 4: Trek to Raminj and overnight stay in a local village

Day - 5: Trek to Rupal and overnight stay in a local village

Day - 6: Trek to Rakaposhi base camp and overnight stay in a camp

Day - 7: Rest day at Rakaposhi base camp and acclimatization

Day - 8: Trek back to Rupal and overnight stay in a local village

Day - 9: Trek back to Raminj and overnight stay in a local village

Day - 10: Trek back to Minapin and drive to Gilgit

Day - 11: Fly back to Islamabad and transfer to hotel

Day - 12: Sightseeing in Islamabad

Day - 13: Departure from Islamabad
