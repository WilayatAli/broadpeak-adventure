OverView

Explore the stunning Haramosh La Trek with Broad Peak Adventures. This adventure takes you through the rugged beauty of the Karakoram Range, where you'll encounter towering peaks, glittering glaciers, and crystal-clear lakes.

From the bustling streets of Islamabad to the tranquil shores of Kutwal Lake, every moment of this trek is filled with wonder and excitement. Along the way, you'll meet friendly locals, camp under the stars, and share stories with fellow adventurers.

As you trek through rough paths and winding trails, you'll be surrounded by majestic mountains, flowing glaciers, and clear mountain lakes. Every step reveals a new breathtaking view, stretching far into the distance. Whether you're exploring the lively streets of Islamabad or relaxing by the peaceful Kutwal Lake, every part of this journey is filled with excitement and awe.

At the summit of Haramosh La, standing tall at 4800 meters, you'll be treated to breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. It's a moment of triumph and awe, a reminder of the power and beauty of nature.

Join us for an unforgettable adventure on the Haramosh La Trek with Broad Peak Adventures.

Itinerary

Day - 1: Arrive in Islamabad, Pakistan and transfer to hotel for a relaxing evening after your journey.

Day - 2: : Fly to Skardu or drive to Chilas (depending on weather conditions and preferences).

Day - 3: Enjoy a leisurely day in Skardu, exploring the local markets or taking in the scenic beauty of the surroundings.

Day - 4: Begin your trek from Skardu to Arandu (2770 meters), immersing yourself in the natural wonders of the region.

Day - 5: : Continue your trek from Arandu to Bukhum, where you'll camp overnight under the starlit sky.

Day - 6: Trek from Bukhum to Khurmala, soaking in the tranquility of the wilderness as you journey onward.

Day - 7: Trek from Khurmala to Bolucho (3800 meters), reaching higher altitudes and experiencing breathtaking vistas along the way.

Day - 8: Trek to Spantik Base Camp, where you'll set up camp amidst the rugged beauty of the mountains.

Day - 9: Continue your trek to Laila Base Camp, marveling at the towering peaks that surround you.

Day - 10: Trek to Sharing Camp, enjoying the camaraderie of fellow adventurers as you make your way forward.

Day - 11: Summit Haramosh La (4800 meters), achieving a thrilling accomplishment and savoring panoramic views from the peak.

Day - 12: : Trek to Kutwal Lake, a serene oasis nestled amidst the mountains, where you'll camp overnight.

Day - 13: Take a well-deserved rest day at Kutwal Lake, reflecting on your journey and soaking in the serenity of the surroundings.

Day - 14: Trek back to Dassu and drive to Chilas, retracing your steps with memories of your expedition fresh in your mind.

Day - 15: Drive back to Islamabad, relishing the scenic landscapes as you make your way back to the capital city.

Day - 16: : Attend a farewell meeting in Islamabad before your departure, reminiscing about your adventure and bidding farewell to new friends before your flight out
