Our Teams

Broad Peak Adventures thrives on a diverse team. Local experts from Gilgit-Baltistan, who initiated this sustainable venture, guide you with intimate knowledge of the region, mountains and culture. Complementing our local team are seasoned representatives from around the world. Each member brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise gained through national and international tourism and hospitality careers. This diverse perspective ensures we cater to the needs of travellers from all corners of the globe. This unique blend offers unparalleled local expertise and international experience for your unforgettable adventure.
