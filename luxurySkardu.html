OverView

Experience the epitome of luxury and indulge in a once-in-a-lifetime journey through the captivating landscapes of Skardu. Our Luxury Skardu Tour is meticulously crafted to offer you an unforgettable experience that combines opulent accommodations, cultural immersion, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Relish the luxurious comforts of your accommodations as you stay at the renowned Shangrila Resort, nestled on the banks of the tranquil Lower Kachura Lake. Enjoy a boat ride on the serene turquoise waters, surrounded by majestic mountains, immersing yourself in the serenity of the surroundings.

This tour is for people who want to explore Skardu with luxury. This tour is perfect for those who value their time and experience and want to make the most of their experience by exploring more in less time.

Itinerary

Day - 1: Arrival in Skardu

Arrive at the Skardu Airport, where you will be greeted by a representative from your luxury tour operator.
Transfer to your luxurious hotel and check-in.
Take some time to relax and acclimate to the altitude of Skardu.
Enjoy a delicious welcome dinner at the hotel's fine dining restaurant.

Day - 2: Skardu City Tour and Shangrila Resort

After breakfast, embark on a guided tour of Skardu city.
Visit the historic Kharpocho Fort and explore its ancient architecture and breathtaking views of the city.
Head to the famous Manthal Buddha Rock, an ancient rock carving that dates back to the 8th century.
Enjoy a leisurely lunch at a local restaurant, sampling traditional Balti cuisine. In the afternoon, visit the enchanting Shangrila Resort, located on the banks of Lower Kachura Lake.
Take a relaxing boat ride on the lake and soak in the picturesque surroundings. Return to your hotel for a sumptuous dinner and overnight stay.

Day - 3: Sadpara Lake - Deosai - Sheosar Lake

i- Deosai National Park: we will wake up early and have a heavy breakfast, then we will depart towards Deosai.
Explore the stunning landscapes of Deosai, known as the "Land of Giants," featuring vast meadows, crystal-clear lakes, and snow-capped mountains.
Enjoy a picnic lunch amidst the pristine natural beauty of the park.
Spot wildlife, including Himalayan brown bears, ibex, and various bird species.

ii- Sadpara Lake: we will make a short stay at Sadpara Lake and move on towards Deosai. Reach Bara Pani and will have lunch. (2 hours)

iii- Sheosar Lake Will drive through Green Meadows towards Sheoshar Lake, visit Sheoshar Lake and drive back to hotel. Dinner and overnight stay.

Return to Skardu in the evening and indulge in a luxurious dinner at your hotel.

Day - 4: Sarfaranga Cold Desert - Shigar Valley and Shigar Fort

After breakfast, check out from your hotel in Skardu and proceed to Shigar Valley. Arrive at the luxurious Serena Shigar Fort Residence, your accommodation for the day. Explore the picturesque Shigar Valley, known for its orchards, traditional architecture, and serene ambiance.
Visit the 17th-century Shigar Fort, a beautiful heritage site that has been converted into a museum and hotel.
Enjoy a guided tour of the fort, learning about its history and cultural significance. Indulge in a delightful lunch at the fort's restaurant, featuring local delicacies. Spend the afternoon

Day - 5: Khaplu Valley and Khaplu Palace

After breakfast, embark on a full-day excursion to the beautiful Khaplu Valley. Explore the historical town of Khaplu and visit the stunning Khaplu Palace, a restored 19th-century palace that offers panoramic views of the valley.
Enjoy a guided tour of the palace, admiring its intricate architecture and cultural artifacts. Indulge in a traditional Balti lunch at a local restaurant.
Visit the Chaqchan Mosque, an ancient mosque known for its architectural beauty and cultural significance.
Return to Skardu in the evening and enjoy a luxurious dinner at your hotel.

Day - 6: Skardu Free Day and Leisure Activities

Today is a free day for you to relax and engage in leisure activities of your choice. Take a stroll along the riverside and enjoy the scenic beauty of Skardu.
Visit local markets and shop for traditional handicrafts, gemstones, and souvenirs. Alternatively, you can choose to go on a guided hike or explore the city's surrounding areas.
In the evening, enjoy a special farewell dinner at a fine dining restaurant, accompanied by traditional music and dance performances.

Day: 7: Depart from Skardu with some special souvenirs
